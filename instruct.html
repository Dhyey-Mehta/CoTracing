  • How to use

  1. Visit Google Takeout and login with your Google account

  2. Locate "Location History" and make sure that the check mark next to it is selected

  3. Follow through with the rest of the steps as displayed on screen

  4. When the takeout is done, you should receive an email to download the contents of the takeout within a few minutes

  5. Download and extract the archive

  6. Locate the .json file for the month you want to test under /Location History/Semantic Location History/2020

  7. Upload the relevant file to the web service
